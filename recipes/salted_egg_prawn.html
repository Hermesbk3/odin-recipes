Salted Egg Prawn

Salted Egg Prawn is Super Delicious!

The recipe is very simple to make

Ingredients

  • 500g prawns (peeled and deveined, leave tails on)
  • 4 salted egg yolks (steamed and mashed)
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2 tbsp cooking oil
  • 3 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 2 red chilies (sliced, optional)
  • 10 curry leaves
  • 1 tbsp evaporated milk
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • ½ tsp salt (adjust to taste)
  • ½ tsp white pepper
  • Cornstarch (for coating)

Steps to Cook Lasagna

  1. Coat prawns lightly with cornstarch and deep-fry or pan-fry until crispy. Set aside.
  2. Heat butter and oil in a pan, then sauté garlic, chilies, and curry leaves until fragrant.
  3. Add mashed salted egg yolks and stir until foamy.
  4. Pour in evaporated milk, sugar, salt, and white pepper, stirring well.
  5. Toss in fried prawns and coat evenly with the sauce.
  6. Serve hot and enjoy!
