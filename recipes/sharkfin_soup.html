Sharkfin Soup

Sharkfk Soup is a Chinese Delicacy!

The recipe is very simple to make

Ingredients

  • 50g dried shark fin (or ethical alternatives like imitation shark fin or shredded chicken)
  • 1L chicken stock (or superior broth)
  • 100g crab meat (optional)
  • 50g dried scallops (soaked & shredded)
  • 2 dried shiitake mushrooms (soaked & sliced)
  • 1 egg white (beaten)
  • 1 tbsp Shaoxing wine
  • 1 tbsp light soy sauce
  • ½ tsp white pepper
  • 1 tbsp cornstarch (mixed with 2 tbsp water for thickening)
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • Chopped green onions & cilantro (for garnish)

Steps to Cook Sharkfin Soup

1. Prepare the Shark Fin:

• Soak the dried shark fin in water overnight.
• Simmer in boiling water for 30 minutes until softened. Drain and rinse.

2. Make the Broth:

• In a pot, bring the chicken stock to a boil.
• Add soaked and shredded scallops, shiitake mushrooms, and crab meat. Simmer for 10 minutes.

3. Add Seasoning:

• Stir in Shaoxing wine, soy sauce, white pepper, and sesame oil.

4. Thicken the Soup:

• Slowly pour in the cornstarch slurry while stirring until the soup thickens.

5. Add Shark Fin & Egg White:

• Gently add prepared shark fin. Let it simmer for another 5 minutes.
• Slowly drizzle the beaten egg white into the soup while stirring for a silky texture.

6. Final Touch & Serve:

• Garnish with chopped green onions and cilantro.
• Serve hot with a side of black vinegar for extra flavor.
